About Us

The pandemic that is ravaging the world over, has not been any less disruptive for the students and teaching facility across the Nation. In light of the fact that the country is being affected by this pandemic , causing even more misery and suffering to the underprivileged. In Kenya most underprivileged students are faced with a lot of Challenges for example Lack of proper uniform, expensive textbooks and for Higher education levels, exorbitant fees structures. To put this into perspective 42% of children under 18 live in monetarily poor households. As Swami Vivekananda once said, "Education is the movement from darkness to light". Imagine being in the dark for more than a year.

Nearly 1 in 3 children live in households lacking the minimum of nationally agreed financial resources and are deprived of a minimum of 3 or more basic needs and services. 23% of children are multidimensionally poor even though they live in households that are not monetarily poor, indicating that there are issues with service accessibility, affordability, and availability. Orphans, children with disability, children in rural areas, and those who have more siblings are more likely to be multidimensionally poor. Children residing in communities that do not have access to basic services like healthcare – in terms of distance – are more likely to be multidimensionally poor Children residing in areas that experience climatic shocks are more likely to be multidimensionally poor.

How We Help

In order to support the underprivileged, we have devised a revolutionary website that will provide a stage to voice the needs of our future world leaders, engineers, doctors and computer scientists. Our core idea is to connect the fortunate and the misfortunate to help each other be the best of version of themselves. This is conveyed by letting schools, churches, children's homes and boarding schools to express their necessities for a better learning experience. On the other hand, the generous people, enjoy the heart-warming moments where their small contribution helped someone immensely.

Secondly, with the current pandemic the necessity of online education has increased through our donor platform not only funding can be provided but also various other resources can be provided to support and improve the accessibility of information, e.g., Online courses, devices for online education etc. The key reason as to why our project will be a success and what makes our solution special is the journey that the user will enjoy and the connection between the donor and receiver provided via our platform. Together with the vast applicability of the project is beyond anyone's thinking.
